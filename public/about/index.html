About Your Own World

A focused home for stories that deserve one.

Your Own World is an all-in-one worldbuilding and writing platform built for writers who need more than a blank document — and less than an overwhelming wiki system.

Why we built it

Writers working on complex stories — fantasy novels, D&D campaigns, sci-fi series, historical epics — have always faced the same problem: the story exists in too many places at once. The manuscript is in one app. The characters are in a spreadsheet. The lore is in a notebook. The maps are pinned to a wall. The timeline is in a separate document.

Every time a writer switches between these tools, they lose flow. Every time they forget to update one, they introduce an inconsistency. Every time they search for a detail they know they wrote somewhere, they lose precious writing time.

Your Own World exists to solve that problem. One workspace. Everything connected. Nothing lost.

What makes it different

Writing-first

Most worldbuilding tools treat writing as secondary. Your Own World treats the manuscript as the centre of gravity — the worldbuilding tools exist to serve the story, not the other way around.

Everything connected

Characters link to scenes. Lore links to characters and locations. The timeline links to events that shaped the world. Your world isn't a collection of documents — it's a connected whole.

Simpler by design

We deliberately chose not to include every possible feature. Your Own World is comprehensive enough to run a complex project and simple enough that you can open it and immediately start working.

AI that knows your world

The AI assistant reads your actual project data — not a generic creative prompt system, but a context-aware collaborator that knows your characters, chapters, and lore before you ask it anything.

What's in it

Your Own World is organised into six workspaces:

  • Draft Full manuscript editor with acts, chapters, scenes, word count tracking, and writing goals.
  • Plan Story outline, beat planning, ideas board, and writing schedule.
  • Cast Character profiles, relationship mapping, family trees, and faction management.
  • World Lore encyclopedia, location manager, interactive map builder, timeline, and world history.
  • Track Project dashboard with word count, pacing graphs, and progress tracking.
  • Assist Context-aware AI writing assistant that knows your actual project.

Built by writers, for writers

Your Own World is built by a small team that writes. We use the platform ourselves — for novels, for campaigns, for world-building projects that got out of hand in the best possible way.

Every feature in Your Own World exists because it solved a real problem we had, or because writers using the platform asked for it. We publish a public roadmap and take feature requests seriously. This is an ongoing project, not a finished product — and the community of writers using it shapes what it becomes.

Contact & support

Questions, feedback, or support requests: support@yourownworld.co.uk. Feature requests can also be submitted directly inside the app using the Feature Request tab in the About section.
