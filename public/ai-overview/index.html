AI Writing Assistant

An AI that actually knows your story.

The AI assistant in Your Own World isn't a generic creative chatbot. It has access to your actual project — your characters, chapters, lore entries, locations, and world notes — and uses that context to give you help that's genuinely relevant to the story you're writing.

The difference context makes

Most AI writing tools know nothing about your project. You have to re-explain your characters, your world, your tone — every single time. The YOW AI assistant doesn't work like that.

Generic AI writing tools

You paste in a description of your character and ask for help. The AI responds based only on what you just told it. It doesn't know the character's history. It doesn't know their relationships. It doesn't know the world they live in. The advice is generic because the context is minimal.

Your Own World AI assistant

The AI has already read your character profiles, your lore entries, your world notes, and the chapters you've written. When you ask for help with a scene, it answers knowing who the characters are, what the stakes mean, and what the world's rules say. The help is specific because the context is real.

What you can use the AI assistant for

Scene Writing & Continuation

Ask the AI to continue a scene, write a passage in your established style, or draft dialogue between specific characters. It knows their voices from your existing chapters and character notes.

Character Development

Explore how a character might react to a situation, develop their arc, deepen their backstory, or find the contradiction that makes them interesting. The AI knows what you've already established.

Worldbuilding Support

Ask the AI to help you develop a magic system, create lore for a religion, name a location, or expand the history behind an event. It builds on what already exists in your world.

Consistency Checking

Ask the AI to check whether a plot point contradicts your established lore, or whether a character's action is consistent with their profile. Catch problems before they reach your readers.

Plot & Story Ideas

Stuck on what happens next? The AI can suggest plot directions grounded in your story's existing threads — not generic story advice, but ideas that follow from what you've actually written.

Lore Generation

Need a myth, a legend, a historical account, or an in-world document? The AI can generate lore-consistent content that fits the voice and logic of your world — not a generic fantasy setting.

How it works

Context-aware creativity — in every section of the workspace.

The AI assistant is available throughout Your Own World — not hidden in a separate tab. When you're working on a character profile, the AI knows you're in character mode. When you're in the manuscript editor, it knows which chapter you're in. The context switches with you.

  • Select which characters, chapters, or lore entries to include
  • Available in the manuscript, character profiles, lore, and more
  • Add a custom instruction for tone, style, or specific guidance
  • Responses stay within your world's established rules
  • No re-explaining your world — the AI already knows it
