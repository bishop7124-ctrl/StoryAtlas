Built for Game Masters

Your world is alive. Your notes should be too.

A great homebrew campaign feels alive because the world reacts, remembers, and evolves. That only happens when the DM has their world properly organised. Your Own World makes that organisation effortless.

Document your world before the campaign starts. Record what happens session by session. Build the network of NPCs, locations, and lore that makes your world feel three-dimensional. When players go off-script, you'll have the context you need to improvise consistently.

  • Track NPC relationships and allegiances
  • Record session outcomes and how the world changes
  • Manage secrets — what players know vs what's hidden
  • Family trees for noble houses and dynasties
  • World history that predates the campaign
  • AI assistant for brainstorming encounters and lore
