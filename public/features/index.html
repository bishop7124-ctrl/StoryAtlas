Six workspaces. One story world.

Your Own World is organised into six focused areas, each designed to handle a core part of the storytelling process.

✍

Draft

A full manuscript editor built for long-form writing. Organise your story into acts, chapters, and scenes. Track word counts, set writing goals, and monitor your pacing — all within a clean, distraction-reduced interface.

🗂

Plan

Map your story before you write it. Use the outline builder to plan arcs and beat structures. Capture ideas on the board before they escape. Schedule writing sessions with the calendar. Build the roadmap your story needs.

👤

Cast

Every character your story needs, properly documented. Full dossiers with appearance, backstory, goals, and flaws. Visual relationship mapping between characters. Family trees to track lineage. Faction management for guilds, houses, and organisations.

🌍

World

The living atlas of your story world. Build interactive maps with pins and annotations. Manage locations with detailed notes. Maintain a lore encyclopedia. Record your world's timeline and history. Everything your world contains, in one place.

📊

Track

Know exactly where your project stands. The dashboard shows word count by chapter, overall progress against goals, pacing analysis, and project momentum. Stop guessing how close you are to done — see it clearly.

🤖

Assist

An AI writing assistant that knows your actual project. Not a generic chatbot — a context-aware creative collaborator that has read your characters, chapters, lore, and world notes. Always available, never in the way.

Write your story from first word to final chapter.
