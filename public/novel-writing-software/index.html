For Novelists

Stop juggling tools. Start finishing novels.

The average novelist uses four to six separate tools: a word processor, a character spreadsheet, a notes app, a world-building wiki, a timeline tool, and maybe an outline document. Every time you switch between them, you lose flow and risk inconsistency.

Your Own World replaces all of them. Your manuscript, characters, world, outline, and notes all live in the same workspace, all linked to each other. Switch from writing a scene to checking a character detail without leaving the platform.

  • No more switching between apps mid-session
  • Characters link directly to the scenes they appear in
  • Lore is searchable from anywhere in the workspace
  • Timeline keeps your chronology consistent
  • Word count goals and pacing keep you on track
