Frequently asked pricing questions

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. The free plan requires no payment details. Create an account and start building your first project immediately.

What counts as an "active project"?

On the free plan, one project is fully editable at any time. Additional projects you create can be read and viewed, but editing requires selecting one as your active project or upgrading to Pro for unlimited simultaneous editing.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel a Pro subscription at any time and you'll retain Pro access until the end of your billing period. Your projects and data are never deleted when you downgrade.

Is my data safe if I downgrade?

Yes. All your projects and their data are preserved. On the free plan, you can read all your projects — you'll just need to pick one as your editable active project.
