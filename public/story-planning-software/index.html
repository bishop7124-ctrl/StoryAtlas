Every planning tool you need — connected to your manuscript

Planning tools are only useful if they're linked to the story you're actually writing. In Your Own World, everything you plan flows directly into the workspace where you write.

📋

Story Outline Builder

Structure your novel from the top down. Map acts, sequences, and scenes. Reorder sections with ease. Build the skeleton of your story before you write a single word of prose.

🎯

Beat Planning

Work with story beats and turning points. Plan inciting incidents, midpoints, dark nights, and climaxes. Use proven narrative structures as a scaffold — or build your own.

💡

Ideas Board

Capture ideas the moment they arrive. The ideas board is an open, freeform space for fragments, scene concepts, dialogue snippets, and creative sparks — nothing gets lost.

📅

Writing Schedule

Plan your writing sessions with the built-in calendar. Set daily or weekly targets. Track your streaks. Treat writing like the commitment it is — with a schedule to match.

📊

Progress Tracking

Monitor word counts, chapter completion, and overall progress against your goal. Pacing graphs show whether your chapters are balanced or front-loaded. Know exactly where you stand.

🔗

Connected to Everything

Your outline links to your characters, lore, and timeline. Planning a scene? Check the character note. Reference the timeline. Your plan and your world are always in sync.

Why it matters

A plan catches plot holes before they cost you 60,000 words.

The most painful rewrites come from structural problems that a solid outline would have caught. Story planning software doesn't constrain your creativity — it protects it. When you know where your story is going, you write with more confidence, more speed, and fewer dead ends.
